Emirates Group Roblox

I founded Emirates Group Roblox to bring aviation to life virtually for teenagers and aspiring professionals around the world. Our goal is to connect aviation enthusiasts globally and allow them to experience the world of flight operations, customer service, and airport management in a realistic and educational environment.

Through this platform, I’ve developed skills in leadership, management, communication, and digital infrastructure — all essential for anyone looking to build a career in the aviation industry.
