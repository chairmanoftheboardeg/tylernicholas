About Me

I’m a high school student at École Française des Seychelles, studying with CNED and specialising in Mathematics, English, and Physics-Chemistry. I’m a highly driven and passionate individual who enjoys exploring professional opportunities and discovering how different careers operate. My dream is to become a commercial pilot — particularly to one day fly the Boeing 777-300ER, my favourite aircraft. I’m deeply fascinated by how aviation works in every aspect, from aerodynamics to flight operations. I also enjoy serving as an Air Traffic Controller on realistic virtual networks such as PFATC, ATC24, and VATSIM.

Emirates Group Roblox

I founded Emirates Group Roblox to bring the real-world experience of aviation into a virtual space — connecting young aviation enthusiasts around the globe and offering a platform to learn, collaborate, and share their passion for flight. Our goal is to inspire teenagers who dream of working in aviation to experience what it’s like to be part of an airline and airport operation — virtually, yet professionally.
