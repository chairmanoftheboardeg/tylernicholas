About Me

Hello! I’m Tyler Nicholas, a high school student at École Française des Seychelles currently studying with CNED. My chosen specialities are Mathematics, Physics-Chemistry, and English. I’m deeply curious about the mechanics of flight, aircraft systems, and how every component works together to make aviation one of the most fascinating fields in the world.

My dream is to become a Commercial Pilot and fly long-haul aircraft such as the Boeing 777-300ER — a jet I’ve admired for years. Beyond flying, I’m also interested in air traffic control, flight operations, and the business side of airlines. I regularly participate in aviation simulation networks such as PFATC, ATC24, and VATSIM, where I can develop realistic communication and coordination skills.

I love learning, exploring new career opportunities, and working in environments that encourage professionalism and creativity. Aviation is not just my passion — it’s my purpose.
